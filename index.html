About the Advocate

Kiran Thirumalesh J. is a dedicated advocate focused on protecting his clients' rights with clear, results-driven legal guidance.

Every matter is handled with care, confidentiality and a strong sense of responsibility. With a foundation of B.Com, LL.M and PGDFA and 9+ years of experience, clients trust Kiran for honest, practical counsel.

  • Guest Faculty at BMS College of Law and Jain College of Law.
  • Worked at Lex Plexus (2018–2019) as Associate to Hon'ble Sitting Judge C. M. Poonacha, High Court of Karnataka.
  • Worked 6+ years at HSD Associates, Basavanagudi, Bengaluru.
  • Committed to honesty, ethics and the client's best interest in every matter.
